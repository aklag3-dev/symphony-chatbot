About Symphony Assist

Symphony Assist is the 24/7 driver support assistant for Symphony Fleet Management. It answers questions about handovers, charging, reimbursements, vehicle condition, and emergencies.

Questions are answered from the official Symphony driver policy, matched locally in your browser. When a question is outside the policy and an AI fallback is enabled by Symphony, the request is sent to a secure Symphony server which consults Gemini on your behalf. Your browser never sees the AI key.
